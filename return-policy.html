Return & Refund Policy

At Spice Nutrition, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality gym supplements and nutrition products. If you are not fully satisfied with your purchase, our Return & Refund Policy ensures a smooth and transparent process. Please read the policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. Eligibility for Returns

  • Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery.
  • Products must be unopened, unused, and in their original packaging.
  • Items that have been opened, used, damaged, or tampered with are not eligible for return due to health and safety reasons.
  • Certain items such as clearance products, promotional items, or free samples cannot be returned.

2. Return Process

  • To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team with your order number, product details, and reason for return.
  • Once your request is approved, you will receive instructions on how to return the product.
  • Customers are responsible for the shipping costs of returning the product, unless the return is due to a wrong or defective item received.

3. Refunds

  • After the returned product is inspected and approved, a refund will be issued to your original payment method.
  • Refunds are typically processed within 7–10 business days after the returned product is received.
  • Shipping charges, handling fees, and cash-on-delivery charges (if applicable) are non-refundable.

4. Damaged or Wrong Products

  • If you receive a defective, damaged, or incorrect product, please contact us immediately within 48 hours of delivery.
  • We will arrange for a replacement or issue a full refund at no additional cost to you.
  • Supporting images/videos of the damaged or incorrect product may be required for verification.

5. Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened supplement containers.
  • Products without original seals, packaging, or labels.
  • Gift cards, vouchers, and promotional freebies.

6. Cancellation Policy

You have the right to:

  • Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped.
  • Once an order is dispatched, it cannot be cancelled, but you may still be eligible for a return (as per the conditions above).
